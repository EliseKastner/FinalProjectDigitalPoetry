Uncertainty

Why not leave the world to chance?
Sublime as she was filled to sing-
Swift blown across the hilly wood,
Both smile and trouble in their dapper dew.
Lead her forth to thy glittering head;
Sunbeams will kiss her face
Rich with fresh verdure and adornment;
Hold calm as the world comes to an end.
