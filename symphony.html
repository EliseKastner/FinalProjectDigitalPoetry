Symphony

of silence
Softly the silence swells and weaves,
A symphony of peace that weaves and breathes.
The sound is serene, the spirit free
A blanket of solace for you and me.
No need for words or a sound to be heard,
The music of emptiness is its own reward.
The soul is at rest, the spirit content
The essence of stillness, the peace it is sent.
The silence is broken, yet still it remains,
A universal language, a calming refrain.
The soul is refreshed, the spirit renewed,
The symphony of silence brings peace anew.
