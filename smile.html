Smile

The curve of your smile haunts my memory
Rose a new love song after morn;
Your heart holds me to its motion,
Blinded and scented with radiant sunshine;
Night me with the night upon me,
Entwines me with her eerie smile,
Sunshine and darkness into night's wide warp.
