Anthology of Sound

Elise Kastner
Spring 2023

Welcome to my COM369 final project. I created an anthology of 15 poems, both AI-generated and original
compositions and transposed those poems into song using MIDI sounds in Ableton Live 11 music software.
I took some creative liberties with how I played the poems and with their sounds in an
attempt to match the theme and feeling of the poems themselves.

The first 5 pieces are completely AI generated, the next 5 used Verse by Verse and my input
to write the pieces, and the final 5 are my own original compositions.

You can use the navigation bar to go through the anthology to hear and read each piece.
Enjoy!!
